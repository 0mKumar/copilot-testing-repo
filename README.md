# Pikachu

Pikachu is an Electric-type Pokémon introduced in Generation I. It is one of the most popular and recognizable Pokémon in the franchise.

## Characteristics
- **Type**: Electric
- **Species**: Mouse Pokémon
- **Height**: 0.4 m (1'04")
- **Weight**: 6.0 kg (13.2 lbs)
- **Abilities**: Static, Lightning Rod (hidden ability)

## Description
Pikachu stores electricity in the pouches on its cheeks. When it releases pent-up energy in a burst, the electric power is equal to a lightning bolt. It can generate powerful electricity and has a tail shaped like a lightning bolt.

## Evolution
- **Evolves from**: Pichu (when leveled up with high friendship)
- **Evolves into**: Raichu (when exposed to a Thunder Stone)

## Notable Facts
- Pikachu is the mascot of the Pokémon franchise
- In the anime, Ash Ketchum's Pikachu is the most famous Pikachu
- Pikachu's name comes from "pika" (the sound of an electric spark) and "chu" (the sound a mouse makes in Japanese)
